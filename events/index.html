Take Home a Memory

Every guest leaves with a tangible keepsake they'll treasure forever.

Orchestrating the perfect gathering requires a lot of energy, but the goal is always the same: creating an experience your guests will talk about for years. That's where we step in to help!

Imagine sending your friends and family home with a high-quality photo magnet that freezes a moment from your celebration in time. Far better than a standard party favor, this is a keepsake that earns a permanent spot on the fridge and keeps the memory of your big day alive.

From elegant weddings and lively birthday parties to church fellowships and corporate mixers, our live magnet service adds a layer of magic to any occasion. Think of it as the excitement of a photo booth, but with a result that lasts a lifetime.

For our couples tying the knot, we offer a modern twist on the classic guestbook. Rather than a book full of signatures, imagine a collection of photo magnets featuring the smiling faces of everyone who came to support you. 💍
