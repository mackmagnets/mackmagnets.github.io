Book Us for Your Event!

Custom photo magnets made on-site for memorable event keepsakes.

You've planned every detail — the venue, the food, the playlist. Now imagine your guests walking away with a one-of-a-kind photo magnet that captures the spirit of your celebration. Not just a party favor they'll toss in a drawer, but a keepsake they'll proudly display on their fridge and smile at every single day.

Whether it's a wedding reception, a milestone birthday, a baby shower, a quinceañera, a corporate gathering, or a church social — our on-site photo magnet station turns any event into something your guests will never forget. They snap a photo, and minutes later they're holding a vibrant, high-quality custom magnet. It's like a photo booth, but they actually keep the result forever.

For brides, our magnets double as a creative guest book — instead of signatures in a book that collects dust, guests leave their mark with a photo that becomes a magnet you can display in your home. Every glance at your fridge brings back the warmth of the people who celebrated with you.

We bring everything to you — setup, equipment, materials, and a friendly attendant who handles it all. You focus on enjoying your day while we create keepsakes your guests will treasure for years to come.
