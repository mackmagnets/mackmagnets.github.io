Detailed Analysis

What Changed & Why

❌ Old Site Issues
  • ⚠️ Only 1 product listed — limited catalog and revenue potential
  • ⚠️ Basic Shopify template — no custom branding or differentiation
  • ⚠️ No event packages — missing major revenue stream
  • ⚠️ No FAQ page — customers have to contact for basic questions
  • ⚠️ No wholesale page — missing B2B opportunities
  • ⚠️ Minimal SEO — no Schema.org markup, missing meta tags
  • ⚠️ No structured data — poor search engine visibility
  • ⚠️ Limited mobile optimization — generic Shopify responsive
  • ⚠️ No testimonials — lack of social proof
  • ⚠️ No newsletter signup — missing email marketing
  • ⚠️ Shopify dependency — monthly fees, limited control
  • ⚠️ No accessibility features — skip links, ARIA labels missing
✅ New Site Improvements
  • ✅ 12 products across 3 categories with smart filtering
  • ✅ Custom modern design with Mack Magnets branding
  • ✅ 4 event packages with California pricing ($550–$1,800)
  • ✅ Comprehensive FAQ with 14 questions and Schema.org markup
  • ✅ Full wholesale page with tiered pricing and inquiry form
  • ✅ Full SEO: Schema.org, Open Graph, Twitter Cards, meta tags
  • ✅ JSON-LD structured data on every page
  • ✅ Mobile-first responsive design with custom breakpoints
  • ✅ Testimonials from California customers for social proof
  • ✅ Newsletter signup with engaging design
  • ✅ Static site — free GitHub Pages hosting, full control
  • ✅ Full accessibility: skip links, ARIA, keyboard navigation
