About Mack Magnets

Turning your best moments into keepsakes you'll see every single day.

Our Story

From a Simple Idea to a Growing Business

Mack Magnets was born in 2024 from a simple realization: photos trapped in phones are memories forgotten. We wanted to bring those moments to life in a way that's tangible, beautiful, and part of your everyday world.

What started as a passion project in Rancho Cucamonga, California has grown into a women-owned small business dedicated to helping families, couples, and event planners preserve their most cherished memories.

Every magnet we create is more than a product — it's a story, a smile, a moment frozen in time and placed right where you'll see it every day.

📸
"Every photo tells a story. We just make sure it sticks around."
— Anita, Founder
Our Journey

How We Got Here

💡 The Spark — 2024

Looking at hundreds of forgotten phone photos, Anita had an idea: what if those moments could live on your fridge, your desk, your world — every single day?

🏠 First Workshop — 2024

From a small corner of her Rancho Cucamonga home, Anita began testing materials, perfecting print quality, and crafting the first batch of custom magnets for family and friends.

🎉 First Event — 2024

A friend's wedding became the first live event. Guests loved the instant photo magnets, and word spread fast through the Inland Empire community.

🚀 Growing — Today

1,000+ magnets created, 50+ events served, and a growing community of happy customers across California and beyond. What started small now brings smiles nationwide.
