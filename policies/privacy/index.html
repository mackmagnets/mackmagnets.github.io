Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

🔒
Your Privacy Matters We're committed to protecting your personal information. We never sell your data.
📋

Information You Provide

  • Contact Info: Name, email, phone number when you contact us or place an order
  • Order Info: Shipping address, product selections, and payment information
  • Photos: Images you submit for custom magnet creation
  • Communication: Messages sent through our contact form or email
🔍

Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, referring website
  • Device Info: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for site functionality only
⚙️

How We Use Your Info

  • Process and fulfill your magnet orders
  • Communicate about orders, shipping, and your account
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Send newsletter updates (only if you opted in)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations
📸

Your Photos

Photos you submit are used solely for producing your order. We do not share, sell, or use your photos for marketing without explicit consent. Photos are securely stored during production and deleted within 30 days of order completion.

🤝

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Shipping carriers (USPS) to deliver your orders
  • Payment processors to securely handle transactions
  • Legal authorities when required by law
🛡️

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

🏛️

Your Rights (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights
👶

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover such data, we will promptly delete it.

📝

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Privacy Questions?

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, reach out to us.
