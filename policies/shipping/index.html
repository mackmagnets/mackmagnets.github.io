Shipping Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

📦
Standard Shipping starts at $4.99 Custom-made to order · Ships from Rancho Cucamonga, CA
⏱️

Processing Time

All orders are custom-made. Please allow 3–5 business days for production before shipping.

Custom or large orders may require additional processing time — we'll keep you updated via email.

🚚

Rates & Delivery

Standard Shipping USPS First Class · Under 1 lb From $4.99 5–7 business days
Weight-Based Shipping Orders over 1 lb Varies by weight Based on package weight & location

Shipping times are estimates from the date of shipment, not the date of order.

📍

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. Track your order directly through the USPS website.

🇺🇸

Shipping Locations

We ship to all 50 US states. For international shipping inquiries, please contact us for a custom quote.

📦

Wholesale & Bulk

Orders of 50+ units receive priority processing and may qualify for discounted or free shipping. Visit our wholesale page for details.

🛡️

Lost or Damaged

If your package arrives damaged or is lost in transit, contact us within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We'll work with the carrier to resolve the issue and send a replacement if needed.

We're here to help with anything shipping-related.
